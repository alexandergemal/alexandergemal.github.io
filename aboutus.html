Oswego Towers is a world renowned Hotel and spa known for its amazing views, service, and memorable experiences. We not only have 56 floors, but each one of those floors brings something new and exciting to the overall experience. From our multiple spas located on the 10th and 55th floor, to the three 5-star restaurants located on the 3rd, 33rd and 56th floor, the the swimming pools and helipads located around the the tower.

Built on the iconic landmar Haystack Rock, with a private bridge to boot, Oswego Towers is like nothing else on the Oregon coastline.
